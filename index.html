Iced Mocha

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 1 serving
Ingredients Quantity
milk 1/4 cup
sugar 1-2 tbsp
cocoa powder 1 tbsp
cold coffee 1 cup
heavy cream 1 tbsp

Step 1: combine 2 tbsp of milk, sugar, and cocoa powder over medium heat.

Step 2: remove from heat and whisk in cold coffee and the remaining milk and heavy cream

Step 3: pour over a cup of ice and top with whipped cream and chocolate syrup if you want!
